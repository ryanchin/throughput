# Getting Started with Product Studio

Product Studio gives enterprise product teams a connected AI-powered workflow that spans the entire product development lifecycle.

## Who Is Product Studio For?

Product Studio is built for enterprise product organizations — VPs of Product, Directors, Senior PMs, Program Managers, Engineering Leaders, and CX teams who need to move faster without sacrificing quality or consistency.

## Core Concepts

### Flows

Every capability in Product Studio is called a **flow**. Each flow is a self-contained tool that solves a specific product management challenge — from goal setting to release readiness.

### Sessions

When you use a flow, you create a **session**. Sessions save your work automatically, are fully editable, and can be revisited at any time.

### Connections

Flows are designed to connect. The output from one flow can be imported as context into another. For example:
- Goals → Scope Definition → Ideation → Feasibility → PRD → Stories

### Knowledge Bases

Upload your organization's documents — strategy decks, research reports, coding standards — and every flow in Product Studio can pull context from them to generate smarter, more relevant output.
