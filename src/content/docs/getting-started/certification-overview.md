# AAVA Certification Overview

AAVA certifications are stackable credentials that demonstrate your proficiency in the AAVA product management methodology.

## Certification Tiers

| Tier | Name | Prerequisites | Badge |
|------|------|--------------|-------|
| 1 | AAVA Foundations | None | Silver |
| 2 | AAVA Practitioner | Foundations | Cyan |
| 3 | AAVA Specialist | Practitioner + 1 Domain | Gold |
| Domain | AAVA [Domain] Expert | Practitioner | Gold |

## How It Works

1. **Create a free account** at [aava.ai/certifications](https://throughput.aava.ai/certifications)
2. **Study the material** — all learning content is free and publicly accessible
3. **Take the exam** — timed, randomized question pool, 80% passing score
4. **Earn your certificate** — shareable on LinkedIn with public verification

## Exam Format

- Questions are randomly selected from a larger pool
- Mix of multiple-choice and open-ended questions
- Open-ended questions are graded by AI
- 80% passing score (higher than the 70% for internal training courses)
- Maximum 3 attempts per 30 days

## LinkedIn Integration

Earned certificates include a one-click "Add to LinkedIn" button that pre-fills your Licenses & Certifications section. Each certificate has a public verification URL.
