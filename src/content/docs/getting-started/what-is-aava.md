# What is AAVA?

AAVA (AI-Accelerated Value Alignment) is a product management methodology that integrates AI-powered agentic workflows into every stage of the product development lifecycle.

## Core Principles

1. **Value Alignment** — Every decision maps back to measurable business outcomes
2. **AI Acceleration** — Leverage LLM-powered agents to automate repetitive PM tasks
3. **Continuous Learning** — Built-in feedback loops that improve processes over time
4. **Stakeholder Transparency** — Automated reporting and real-time visibility

## The AAVA Lifecycle

The methodology covers seven core phases:

| Phase | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| Goals & OKRs | Setting direction and measurable objectives |
| Research & Discovery | Understanding users, market, and competition |
| Ideation & Prioritization | Generating and ranking opportunities |
| Roadmapping | Planning timelines, dependencies, and milestones |
| Sprint Planning | Breaking work into actionable sprints |
| Execution & Tracking | Monitoring progress and managing risks |
| Retrospective & Learning | Reflecting and improving |

Each phase includes AI-powered flows that accelerate traditionally manual PM work.
