# Goals & OKRs

## Overview

The Goals & OKRs phase is the foundation of the AAVA methodology. It ensures that every product decision traces back to measurable business outcomes.

This phase covers three core flows:

1. **Goal Extraction** — Deriving clear product goals from business strategy and stakeholder input
2. **OKR Decomposition** — Breaking goals into measurable Objectives and Key Results
3. **Alignment Validation** — Ensuring OKRs align across teams and with company-level strategy

## Why This Matters

Without clear, measurable goals, product teams drift. The AAVA approach uses AI to accelerate goal-setting while maintaining strategic alignment.

## Inputs

- Business strategy documents
- Stakeholder interviews / notes
- Previous quarter OKR results

## Outputs

- Validated product goals
- Structured OKR hierarchy
- Alignment score across teams
