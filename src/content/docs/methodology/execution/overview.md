# Execution & Tracking

## Overview

The Execution & Tracking phase monitors sprint progress and surfaces risks early. AAVA automates status reporting and risk identification to keep teams focused on building.

This phase covers three core flows:

1. **Progress Tracking** — Automated tracking of sprint and OKR progress
2. **Risk Identification** — AI-powered early warning for delivery risks
3. **Stakeholder Updates** — Generated status reports for leadership

## Inputs

- Sprint backlog status
- Burndown/burnup data
- Team standup notes

## Outputs

- Progress dashboards
- Risk alerts with mitigation suggestions
- Stakeholder-ready status reports
