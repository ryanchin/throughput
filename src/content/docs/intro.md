# Welcome to Product Studio

Product Studio is the AI-powered operating system for enterprise product teams. From strategic planning to shipped software, every tool is designed to help your organization move faster, maintain consistency at scale, and eliminate the busywork that slows product delivery.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Goals & Measurements** — SMART goals, OKRs, and KPIs from a single form
- **Scoping** — Define what's in, what's out, and what's deferred
- **Research** — Market intelligence and competitive analysis
- **Ideation** — Generate and score solution ideas at scale
- **Feasibility** — Technical feasibility and financial business cases
- **Roadmapping** — Capacity-matched roadmaps and audience-tailored presentations
- **Customer Experience** — Research studies and customer journey mapping
- **Backlog Authoring** — PRDs, epics, features, and user stories
- **Development** — Code generation, sprint tracking, and release documentation
- **Testing** — Defect management and release readiness scoring
- **Resources** — AI-powered codebase chat
- **Integrations** — Jira Cloud and Azure DevOps

## The Product Lifecycle

Product Studio flows are designed to connect. Output from one tool becomes input for the next:

**Goals** → **Scope** → **Ideation** → **Feasibility** → **Business Case** → **Roadmap** → **PRD** → **Stories** → **Code** → **Release**

Start anywhere and go as deep as you need. Use one flow in isolation, or chain them together for a complete product development lifecycle.

Every flow includes AI-powered revision, quality validation, session history, and professional exports. Your work is never lost, always editable, and ready to share with stakeholders at every level.
