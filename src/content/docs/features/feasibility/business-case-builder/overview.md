# Business Case Builder

Builds a comprehensive financial business case — with cost/benefit analysis, three financial scenarios, NPV, IRR, ROI, payback period, and an investment recommendation.

## Questions It Answers

- "Is this feature worth the investment?"
- "What's the expected ROI, and when do we break even?"
- "What does the conservative vs. optimistic financial picture look like?"
- "Which cost or benefit assumptions are we most sensitive to?"

## Example Use Cases

- A VP of Product needs to justify a $500K feature investment to the CFO with a rigorous financial analysis that meets enterprise governance standards.
- A product portfolio team wants to compare the business case for three competing feature ideas to decide where to invest next quarter.
- A Senior PM importing a feasibility analysis wants to automatically convert technical effort estimates into dollar costs and project the financial return for executive review.

## What Makes It Special

- Import directly from Feasibility Analysis to pre-fill development costs
- Override any estimate and watch financials recalculate in real time
- Sensitivity analysis highlights which assumptions matter most — flag the variables that could swing your NPV by more than 10%
