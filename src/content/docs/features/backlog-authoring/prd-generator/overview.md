# PRD Generator

Transforms business requirements into a comprehensive, 10-section Product Requirements Document — with knowledge base context, citations, and professional exports.

## Questions It Answers

- "What should the PRD look like for this feature?"
- "What are the functional and non-functional requirements?"
- "What are the acceptance criteria in Given/When/Then format?"
- "What risks and dependencies should we document?"

## Example Use Cases

- A Senior PM has a half-page feature brief and needs to expand it into a complete PRD before sprint planning.
- A team starting a new initiative wants to generate a first-draft PRD from their ideation and scope definition sessions.
- A VP of Product needs consistent, professional PRDs across the entire product organization and wants a standard format that every PM follows.

## What Makes It Special

- 10 best-practice sections generated automatically
- Attach knowledge bases for context-aware generation with clickable citations
- Import from Ideation, Scope Definition, or Goal sessions
- Every section is editable and individually revisable
- Export to PDF or Word with color-coded sections and citation references
