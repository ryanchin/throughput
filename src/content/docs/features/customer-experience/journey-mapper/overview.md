# Journey Mapper

Creates AI-powered customer journey maps with stages, touchpoints, emotion curves, and pain points — with support for multi-persona and competitive journey mapping.

## Questions It Answers

- "What does the end-to-end customer experience look like?"
- "Where are the biggest pain points in the journey?"
- "How does the experience differ across personas?"
- "How does our journey compare to competitors?"

## Example Use Cases

- A CX team wants to map the enterprise onboarding journey and identify where customers drop off during implementation.
- A product team needs to compare how three different buyer personas (IT Admin, End User, Executive Sponsor) experience the same flow.
- A VP of CX wants a side-by-side view of their customer journey vs. a competitor's to identify competitive advantages.

## Mapping Modes

- **Standard** — Single persona, single journey
- **Multi-Persona** — Compare how different personas experience the same journey
- **Competitive** — Side-by-side comparison of your journey vs. a competitor's

## What Makes It Special

- Tracks whether insights are data-backed or hypothetical
- Pain points are scored by severity and frequency so you can prioritize what to fix
