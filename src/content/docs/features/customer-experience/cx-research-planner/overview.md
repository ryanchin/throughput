# CX Research Planner

Designs end-to-end customer research studies — from method recommendations to ready-to-use interview guides, surveys, and recruiting plans.

## Questions It Answers

- "What research methods should I use to answer this question?"
- "What should my interview guide look like?"
- "How do I recruit the right participants?"
- "What screener questions should I use?"

## Example Use Cases

- A PM wants to validate a new feature concept with enterprise customers but hasn't run user research before and needs guidance on methodology.
- A UX research team needs to quickly spin up a mixed-methods study with interview guides, surveys, and recruiting emails for B2B stakeholders.
- A product team debating two design directions wants to plan a usability study to let enterprise users decide.

## What Makes It Special

- Recommends methods based on your objective, budget, and timeline constraints
- Generates the actual instruments — interview scripts, surveys, and recruiting plans
- B2B and B2C modes tailor everything to your audience
