# Ideation Engine

Generates 15-16 unique, scored solution ideas from a problem statement — clustered by theme and categorized as Quick Wins, Strategic Bets, Incremental improvements, or Moonshots.

## Questions It Answers

- "What are all the ways we could solve this problem?"
- "Which ideas have the best balance of impact and feasibility?"
- "What are the quick wins vs. the big strategic bets?"
- "Are there creative solutions we haven't considered?"

## Example Use Cases

- A product team has identified a churn problem but is stuck on solutions. They feed in the problem statement and get 16 scored ideas ranging from quick UX fixes to platform-level changes.
- A cross-functional team needs rapid ideation around "how might we improve enterprise onboarding" and wants ideas ranked by effort and impact.
- A Head of Product exploring a new product vertical wants to see the full landscape of approaches before committing resources and budget to one direction.

## What Makes It Special

- Every idea is scored on five dimensions: impact, feasibility, effort, strategic fit, and risk
- Ideas are auto-categorized and grouped into thematic clusters so you can spot patterns
- Import context from goals, market research, or scope sessions to ground your ideation in real data
- Results include a prioritized ranking so you can see top ideas at a glance
