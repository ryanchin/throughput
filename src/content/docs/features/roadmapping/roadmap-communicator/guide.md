# How to Use the Roadmap Communicator

This guide walks you through generating audience-tailored roadmap presentations.

## Step 1: Select Your Audience

Choose who you're presenting to:

- **Executives** — Strategic narrative, business impact, high-level timeline
- **Engineers** — Technical breakdown, dependencies, capacity details
- **Sales** — Feature previews, customer value, competitive positioning
- **Support** — What's changing, customer impact, migration notes
- **Customers** — Feature highlights, timelines, value messaging

## Step 2: Set the Tone

Adjust the communication tone to match your audience and the context of the presentation.

## Step 3: Review Generated Content

The system produces audience-tailored:

- **Narrative** — The story of what you're building and why
- **Talking points** — Key messages to deliver
- **Summaries** — Condensed views of the roadmap data

All generated from your existing roadmap — no manual re-creation needed.

## Tips

- Generate the executive version first — it forces you to articulate the strategic narrative
- Never share the engineering-detail version with customers — use the customer-specific output
- Regenerate after roadmap changes — stale communications erode trust
