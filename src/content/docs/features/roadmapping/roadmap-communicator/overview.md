# Roadmap Communicator

Generates audience-tailored roadmap presentations — different messaging for execs, engineers, sales, and customers from the same underlying plan.

## Questions It Answers

- "How do I present this roadmap to the C-suite vs. the engineering team?"
- "What narrative should I use for customer-facing roadmap updates?"
- "What are the key talking points for each audience?"

## Example Use Cases

- A Director of Product has one roadmap but needs three presentations: a strategic narrative for the board, a technical breakdown for engineering leadership, and a feature preview for the enterprise sales team.
- A CPO preparing for a customer advisory board wants audience-appropriate messaging that doesn't over-promise to key accounts.

## What Makes It Special

- Choose your audience (Executives, Engineers, Sales, Support, Customers) and tone
- The system generates tailored narratives, talking points, and summaries from your existing roadmap data
