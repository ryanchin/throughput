# Roadmap Planner

Takes your epics, features, and ideas and turns them into a sequenced, capacity-matched product roadmap — with dependencies, thematic clusters, and milestone markers.

## Questions It Answers

- "Given our team capacity, what can we realistically deliver and when?"
- "What depends on what? What's the optimal sequencing?"
- "How do our planned items cluster into themes?"
- "Where should we set milestones?"

## Example Use Cases

- A Group PM has 20 features from various ideation and scoping sessions and needs to build a realistic quarterly roadmap across 3 delivery teams.
- A Director of Product wants to see how buffer percentage affects delivery timelines before committing to dates with executive stakeholders.
- An organization transitioning from ad-hoc planning to structured roadmapping wants a standardized starting point they can refine across product lines.

## What Makes It Special

- Pull items from multiple sources — feasibility analyses, epics, features, ideation ideas, or custom entries
- Configure team count, velocity, sprint length, and buffer percentage
- The system handles sequencing, dependency detection, and capacity matching automatically
