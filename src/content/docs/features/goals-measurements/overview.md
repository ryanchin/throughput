# Goal & Metric Planner

Turns a business objective into a complete strategic planning package — SMART goals, OKRs (Objectives & Key Results), and KPIs — all from a single form.

## Questions It Answers

- "What should our goals be for this quarter?"
- "How do we measure success for this initiative?"
- "What KPIs should we track, and what are realistic targets?"
- "Are our objectives actually aligned with our strategy?"

## Example Use Cases

- A VP of Product kicks off Q3 planning and needs goals, OKRs, and KPIs for a new growth initiative across multiple product lines — generated in minutes instead of days of back-and-forth.
- A Director of Product Management wants to translate "increase enterprise retention" into measurable objectives with baselines and targets they can present to the executive leadership team.
- A product team lead needs to align their squad's work to company-level strategy and wants to see how their goals ladder up to organizational OKRs.

## What Makes It Special

- Everything is editable — refine a single goal or revise the entire plan with AI
- Built-in quality checks catch misalignment before you share it with stakeholders
- Export to PDF or Word when you're ready to present
- Revising a Key Result automatically updates its linked KPIs to stay aligned
