# Progress Tracker

Real-time sprint tracking with AI-powered blocker detection, risk scoring, and automated status reporting — pulling live data from Jira or Azure DevOps.

## Questions It Answers

- "Is this sprint on track to meet its commitment?"
- "What are the current blockers and risks?"
- "Which items are at risk of not being completed?"
- "What should I report to stakeholders?"

## Example Use Cases

- A Scrum Master wants a real-time view of sprint health that goes beyond Jira's built-in burndown chart — with AI-detected blockers and risk flags.
- A program manager tracking five delivery teams needs a single dashboard that highlights at-risk sprints across the portfolio.
- A VP of Engineering wants automated weekly status reports for each team without chasing PMs for updates.

## What Makes It Special

- Pulls live data from Jira or Azure DevOps — no manual data entry
- AI analyzes sprint patterns to detect blockers before they're formally reported
- Risk scoring on individual items and the overall sprint
- Generates stakeholder-ready status reports with one click
