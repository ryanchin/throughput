# Competitive Analysis

Researches how competitors and alternative solutions tackle the same challenges you're working on — without hours of manual desk research.

## Questions It Answers

- "How are competitors solving this problem?"
- "What's the competitive landscape look like for this feature area?"
- "Where are the gaps we could exploit?"
- "What positioning and pricing strategies are others using?"

## Example Use Cases

- A PM building a new collaboration feature wants to see how Notion, Asana, and Monday approach the same problem before designing their solution.
- A VP of Product Strategy wants to identify underserved areas in the market by mapping competitor feature sets across the competitive landscape.

## What Makes It Special

- Import context from feasibility analyses, business cases, or PRDs to make the research laser-focused on your specific feature area
- Generate PowerPoint decks for stakeholder presentations
