# Market Research Synthesizer

Synthesizes market trends, shifting user expectations, risks, and strategic implications for any problem area — like having a research analyst on demand.

## Questions It Answers

- "What's happening in the market around this problem space?"
- "How are customer expectations changing?"
- "What risks should we be aware of before committing to this direction?"
- "What are the strategic implications for our product?"

## Example Use Cases

- A product team exploring a new feature category wants to understand current market trends and user expectations before investing in ideation.
- A Director of Strategy preparing a quarterly business review needs data-backed market insights organized by confidence level.
- A CPO evaluating market expansion wants to identify risks and shifting customer expectations across enterprise segments.

## What Makes It Special

- Every insight is tagged with a confidence level (High, Medium, Low) and backed by sources
- Filter by confidence to focus on what's most reliable
- Results are organized into clear tabs: trends, expectation shifts, risks, and strategic implications
