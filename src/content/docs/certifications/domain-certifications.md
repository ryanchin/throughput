# Domain Certifications

**Badge:** Gold | **Prerequisites:** AAVA Practitioner

## Overview

Domain certifications recognize deep expertise in specific areas of the AAVA methodology. Each domain certification focuses on one phase of the PM lifecycle and tests advanced, practical knowledge.

## Available Domains

| Domain | Focus Area |
|--------|-----------|
| Goals & OKRs Expert | Advanced goal extraction, OKR decomposition, alignment validation |
| Research Expert | User research synthesis, competitive analysis, market sizing |
| Ideation Expert | Opportunity scoring, feature prioritization, impact mapping |
| Roadmapping Expert | Roadmap generation, dependency analysis, timeline estimation |
| Sprint Planning Expert | Story generation, capacity planning, sprint goal setting |
| Execution Expert | Progress tracking, risk identification, stakeholder updates |
| Retrospective Expert | Retro synthesis, action item tracking, continuous improvement |

## Exam Format

All domain certification exams follow the same structure:

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Duration | 60 minutes |
| Questions | 25 (from a domain-specific pool) |
| Format | Multiple-choice + scenario-based open-ended |
| Passing Score | 80% |
| Cooldown | 24 hours between attempts |

## How Domain Certs Unlock Specialist

Earning any one domain certification, combined with your AAVA Practitioner certification, qualifies you to attempt the [AAVA Specialist](/certifications/specialist) exam.

Multiple domain certifications are encouraged and demonstrate breadth of expertise across the methodology.
