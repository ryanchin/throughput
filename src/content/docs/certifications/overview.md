# AAVA Certifications

AAVA certifications are industry-recognized credentials that validate your proficiency in AI-accelerated product management. They are open to anyone — employees and public alike.

## Certification Structure

AAVA offers a tiered certification system with both sequential and domain-specific tracks:

| Tier | Certification | Prerequisites | Passing Score | Badge |
|------|--------------|--------------|---------------|-------|
| 1 | AAVA Foundations | None | 80% | Silver |
| 2 | AAVA Practitioner | Foundations | 80% | Cyan |
| 3 | AAVA Specialist | Practitioner + 1 Domain | 80% | Gold |
| Domain | AAVA [Domain] Expert | Practitioner | 80% | Gold |

## How Certification Works

### 1. Study

All certification learning material is freely accessible without an account. Browse the methodology documentation and certification-specific study guides at your own pace.

### 2. Register

Create a free account at [throughput.aava.ai/certifications](https://throughput.aava.ai/certifications). You can use email/password or a magic link.

### 3. Take the Exam

Each certification exam is:
- **Timed** — typically 60 minutes
- **Randomized** — questions are drawn from a larger pool, so each attempt is unique
- **Mixed format** — multiple-choice and open-ended questions
- **AI-graded** — open-ended responses are evaluated by an LLM with rubric-based scoring

### 4. Earn Your Certificate

Pass with 80% or higher to earn your certificate. Each certificate includes:
- A unique verification URL
- A one-click "Add to LinkedIn" button
- Open Badges 3.0 compliant metadata for machine verification

## Attempt Limits

- **3 attempts per 30-day period** per certification track
- **24-hour cooldown** between failed attempts
- No limit on total lifetime attempts

## Verification

Every issued certificate has a public verification page at `aava.ai/certifications/verify/[hash]`. This page displays the credential details and can be linked from LinkedIn or any professional profile.
