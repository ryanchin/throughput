# Research & Discovery

## Overview

The Research & Discovery phase ensures product decisions are grounded in evidence. AAVA accelerates traditional research workflows using AI-powered synthesis and analysis.

This phase covers three core flows:

1. **User Research Synthesis** — Aggregating and analyzing qualitative user research
2. **Competitive Analysis** — Mapping the competitive landscape
3. **Market Sizing** — Estimating addressable market and opportunity size

## Inputs

- User interview transcripts
- Survey data
- Competitor product information
- Industry reports

## Outputs

- Research synthesis reports
- Competitive landscape maps
- Market size estimates (TAM/SAM/SOM)
