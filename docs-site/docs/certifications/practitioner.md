# AAVA Practitioner Certification

**Tier:** 2 | **Badge:** Cyan | **Prerequisites:** AAVA Foundations

## Overview

The AAVA Practitioner certification demonstrates hands-on proficiency with the AAVA methodology. It tests your ability to apply AI-accelerated PM workflows in real-world scenarios.

## What You'll Be Tested On

- Applying goal extraction and OKR decomposition in practice
- Conducting AI-assisted research synthesis
- Using opportunity scoring and prioritization frameworks
- Building roadmaps with dependency awareness
- Sprint planning with AI-generated stories
- Execution tracking and stakeholder communication
- Running effective retrospectives with AI synthesis

## Study Resources

- All methodology deep-dive pages in this documentation
- Practical exercises in the Throughput training platform

## Exam Details

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Duration | 60 minutes |
| Questions | 30 (from a pool of 50+) |
| Format | Multiple-choice + open-ended (scenario-based) |
| Passing Score | 80% |
| Cooldown | 24 hours between attempts |

## Next Steps

After earning your Practitioner certification, you can:
- Pursue [Domain Certifications](/certifications/domain-certifications) in specific areas
- Work toward the [AAVA Specialist](/certifications/specialist) certification (requires Practitioner + 1 Domain cert)
