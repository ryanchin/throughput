# AAVA Specialist Certification

**Tier:** 3 | **Badge:** Gold | **Prerequisites:** AAVA Practitioner + 1 Domain Certification

## Overview

The AAVA Specialist certification is the highest tier in the AAVA certification system. It recognizes comprehensive mastery of the AAVA methodology and deep expertise in at least one domain area.

## What You'll Be Tested On

- Advanced application of the full AAVA lifecycle
- Cross-functional alignment and stakeholder management
- Complex scenario analysis requiring multi-phase methodology application
- Strategic decision-making with AI-powered insights
- Mentoring and coaching others in the AAVA methodology

## Prerequisites

You must hold both of the following before attempting the Specialist exam:

1. **AAVA Practitioner** certification (active)
2. At least **one Domain Certification** (e.g., Sprint Planning Expert, Research Expert)

## Exam Details

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Duration | 90 minutes |
| Questions | 40 (from a pool of 60+) |
| Format | Multiple-choice + extended open-ended scenarios |
| Passing Score | 80% |
| Cooldown | 24 hours between attempts |
