# AAVA Foundations Certification

**Tier:** 1 | **Badge:** Silver | **Prerequisites:** None

## Overview

The AAVA Foundations certification validates your understanding of the core AAVA methodology. This is the entry point for all AAVA certifications and covers the fundamental concepts across all seven lifecycle phases.

## What You'll Be Tested On

- The AAVA lifecycle and its seven phases
- Core principles of AI-accelerated product management
- Basic goal-setting and OKR frameworks
- Introduction to each methodology phase
- When and why to use AI-powered PM workflows

## Study Resources

- [What is AAVA?](/getting-started/what-is-aava)
- All methodology overview pages in this documentation

## Exam Details

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Duration | 60 minutes |
| Questions | 30 (from a pool of 50+) |
| Format | Multiple-choice + open-ended |
| Passing Score | 80% |
| Cooldown | 24 hours between attempts |

## Next Steps

After earning your Foundations certification, you can pursue the [AAVA Practitioner](/certifications/practitioner) certification.
